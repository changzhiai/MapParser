## DESCRIPTION

Effortlessly parse Map directions into CSV and KML files. Save your trips, visualize routes on OpenStreetMap or Google Maps, and organize your travel itineraries. Perfect for digital nomads, travel planners, and explorers who need their data everywhere.

Stop struggling with manual data entry from Google Maps. MapParser is the most efficient way to extract location data from Google Maps directions and turn them into actionable files for your favorite travel tools.

## STUNNING FEATURES:

- Instant Parsing: Copy and paste your Google Maps directions link, and let MapParser extract every stop, coordinate, and detail instantly.
- Export to CSV & KML: Perfect for importing into Excel, Google Sheets, or advanced GIS software.
- KML Support: Seamlessly import your routes into "My Maps" or other navigation apps.
- Visualizer: See your routes come to life on OpenStreetMap directly within the app.
- Trip History: Save and organize your past parsed routes for quick access later.
- Cloud Syncing: Securely login with Apple or Google to keep your travel itineraries synced across all your devices.
