## RECENT UPDATES

This version brings significant performance enhancements, a complete redesign of our core informational pages, and a more seamless route-sharing experience between web and mobile.

## ENHANCED MAP VISUALIZATIONS
- **State-Linked Interaction**: We’ve overhauled our state management. Now, when you parse a Google Maps route on the home screen, your data automatically follows you to the Interactive OSM Map and Google Maps Viewer. This resolves issues where map pages appeared empty when navigating between views.

## PREMIUM DOWNLOAD EXPERIENCE
- **New Download Hub**: Visit our redesigned download page featuring a high-end, modern aesthetic inspired by top-tier travel platforms.
- **Custom Branded QR Codes**: We’ve introduced store-specific QR codes (Indigo for iOS, Emerald for Android) to make scanning for mobile downloads faster and more visually intuitive.

## DESIGN AND UI REFINEMENT
- **About Page Overhaul**: The About section has been migrated from a modal to a beautiful, full-page glassmorphism experience.
- **Optimized Mobile Spacing**: We’ve tightened horizontal paddings across all informational pages to provide more screen space for content on phone devices.
- **Consistency and Navigation**: Standardized "Back to Home" navigation at the bottom of all secondary pages for better thumb-reach on mobile. Applied text-justification to all long-form content for a cleaner, magazine-style layout.

## SEO AND CRAWLABILITY BOOST
- **Sitemap 2.0**: Updated our automated sitemap to prioritize download links and new sections.
- **Crawlable SPA Structure**: Implemented a hidden discovery navigation for search engine bots to ensure all your routes and pages are properly indexed.
- **Advanced Structured Data**: Upgraded our JSON-LD schema to help search engines display rich snippets for our iOS and Android apps directly in search results.

## CORE IMPROVEMENTS
- **Native Social Login Integration**: Continued optimizations for native Apple and Google sign-in to bypass mobile WebView restrictions.
- **Z-Index Fixes**: Resolved stacking issues to ensure sign-in and profile modals always appear above the mobile navigation bar.
